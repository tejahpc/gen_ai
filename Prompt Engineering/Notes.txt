Principles:
1. write clear and specific instructions
2. give model time to think

Tactic 1.1: Use delimiters to clearly indicate distinct parts of the input
Tactic 1.2: Ask for a structured output
Tactic 1.3: Ask the model to check whether conditions are satisfied
Tactic 1.4: "Few-shot" prompting
Tactic 2.1: Specify the steps required to complete a task
Tactic 2.2: Instruct the model to work out its own solution before rushing to a conclusion

Summarization: 
- Summarize in {x} words for {role}
- Extract relevant information

Inferring:
- Identify the following from the document

Transforming (e.g, translation)

Expanding (bullet points -> paragraphs)
